My Background

I started my journey in Computer Information Systems a little late in the game. Before switching to CIS my Junior year at the University of Louisville, I was a Political Science major.

It has been an awesome adventure, and I have learned so much about not only IT, but myself.

When I first enrolled into the College of Business as a CIS major, I had never even worked with CSS or HTML. Now, a few years later, just a few of the things I have accomplished include:

  • Created an IT Strategic Assessment for a local non-profit
  • Learn how to use SAS Enterprise Miner
  • Completed 3 internships
  • Used GitHub to collaborate on various projects
  • Worked on a MVC and .NET web solution that uses Bootstrap
  • Learned how to write queries with SQL
  • Applied Systems Analysis and Design concepts to gather system requirements for a client
  • Taken two classes in C# software development

I love to learn, and am so glad I took the opportunity to jump into a field that I knew nothing about to challenge myself!

In the future, I hope to help other women and minorities pursue careers in IT - I feel strongly that a diverse workforce makes companies perform better.
